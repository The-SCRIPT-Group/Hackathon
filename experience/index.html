Experience

Meet us at MIT World Peace University, Kothrud. Founded as MAEER's MIT-Pune in the year 1983, MIT World Peace University is a center for scientific, educational, technological, & spiritual development for the youth. You’ll find lush green lawns, wide roads, and a peaceful atmosphere on the campus.

Venue

MIT-WPU Hackrupt is taking place at MIT World Peace University — the historic site of the 1915 Panama-Pacific Exposition and one of the most photographed buildings in the city. After World War II, it was also a military storage depot, a warehouse for the Parks Department, and even a telephone book distribution center.

Transportation

We recommend using ride-sharing services, buses, or bikes to arrive at MIT World Peace University. There’s limited parking available at the venue, and the surrounding neighbourhood has designated parking allotted for its residents and local businesses.

Ride-sharing

If you’re getting to Universe by Uber or Ola, the address for MIT World Peace University, S.No.124, Paud Road, Kothrud, Pune, Maharashtra 411038. There will be signs at the venue to help the driver drop you off at the right spot.

Buses

Buses may drop students inside the campus, but parking space cannot be guaranteed.
