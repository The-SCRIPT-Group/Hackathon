Venue

For the second year, GitHub Universe is taking place at the Palace of Fine Arts—the historic site of the 1915 Panama-Pacific Exposition and one of the most photographed buildings in the city. After World War II, it was also a military storage depot, a warehouse for the Parks Department, and even a telephone book distribution center.

Transportation

We recommend using ride-sharing services, Muni, or bikes to arrive at The Palace of Fine Arts. There’s no parking available at the venue, and the surrounding neighborhood has limited parking for its residents and local businesses.

Ride-sharing

If you’re getting to Universe by Lyft or Uber, the address for The Palace of Fine Arts is 3301 Lyon Street, San Francisco, CA 94123. There will be signs at the venue to help the driver drop you off in the right spot.

Biking

Free valet bike parking is available at the Palace of Fine Arts.

City guide

There’s plenty to do in San Francisco. If you’re traveling from out of town, we recommend staying a few extra days to explore Northern California.

Where to stay

Most of San Francisco’s hotels are in the Union Square and SoMa neighborhoods. These areas have easy access to public transportation and plenty to do when you aren’t at Universe. Unfortunately, hotel prices can be high, and we recommend Airbnb as an alternative.

If you plan to stay at a hotel, we request that you stay at a socially responsible hotel endorsed by fairhotel.org such as:

  • Intercontinental Mark Hopkins
  • The Clift Royal Sonesta Hotel
  • Hotel Vitale
  • Hotel Zephyr
